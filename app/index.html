About Me

Cori grew up in a small South Florida beach town where she developed her artistic abilities at a very young age. Her parents knew she had the artistic bug when she won 1st place in the Kindergarten art contest. As she went through grade school, art class was always her best grade and favorite class. At home, she was constantly rearranging and re-decorating her room, this drove her parentsâ€™ nuts. Mostly because they were the ones paying for it and painting every time she wanted a change. Thankfully they were supportive in her design choices.

When Cori entered college at the University of Florida she discovered that she could make a career out of doing what she loved. There was a whole world contributing and devoted to designing interior spaces and this was the career path she knew she needed to take. After college, Cori knew she needed to move to a more exciting and design driven city to cultivate her talents. She took a huge leap of faith and made the big move to Chicago. She now has been in the interior design field for 6 years and the fire within her to learn continues to burn. Cori loves helping people and seeing the satisfaction of clients once their space is complete, pulling together spaces that clients need help with, partnering her vision with their needs and wants. Her passion for keeping up with the trends is a daily hobby of hers. She lives for design and is always immersing herself in it from design books and magazines, to the latest social media posts from her favorite designers.
